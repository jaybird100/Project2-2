all lectures:returns all lectures
lectures on <DAY>: returns all lectures from one specific day of the week
all lectures on <DATE>:returns all lectures on a specific date
lectures in <NUM> days: returns all lectures in a specified number of days
lectures today:returns all lectures that are today
lectures tomorrow:returns all lectures that are tomorrow
lectures yesterday:returns all lectures that were yesterday
all <COURSE> lectures: returns all lectures of a specific course
<COURSE> lectures on <DATE>:returns all lectures of a specific course on a specific date
<COURSE> lectures next <DAY>:returns all lectures of a specific course for the next specified day:
Add to-<FILE NAME>-<COURSE>,<TIME>,<DATE>,<DAY>,<EXTRA>
